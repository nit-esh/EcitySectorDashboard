Programme Phase Activities

Phase-wise tasks applicable across all programmes and centres

🌐
Phase 1 — Web Listing & Setup
6–8 weeks before
▼
  • 🔹Raise programme request with Isha national team
  • 🔹Verify listing on isha.sadhguru.org — dates, venue, fee
  • 🔹Confirm registration link live for correct centre
  • 🔹Block hall dates on centre calendar
  • 🔹Identify and form volunteer team — assign roles
  • 🔹Set up fee collection QR / payment link
📣
Phase 2 — Promotion
4–6 weeks before
▼
  • 🔸Create programme poster — date, time, venue, fee
  • 🔸Share in all centre WhatsApp groups with link
  • 🔸Post on Instagram / Facebook
  • 🔸Personalised WhatsApp to eligible past participants
  • 🔸Reminder broadcasts — 3 weeks, 2 weeks, 1 week before
  • 🔸Personal calling — divide list among volunteers
📝
Phase 3 — Registration
2–4 weeks before
▼
  • 🔹Monitor website registrations daily
  • 🔹Handle offline / walk-in registrations
  • 🔹Send confirmation to each registered participant
  • 🔹Manage waitlist if programme is full
  • 🔹Send reminder with map and timing — 2 days before
  • 🔹Prepare printed attendance sheet
🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Phase 4 — Volunteer Briefing
1 week before
▼
  • 🔸Confirm all volunteers — no last-minute dropouts
  • 🔸Briefing session — roles, timings, what to expect
  • 🔸Assign stations clearly
  • 🔸Share volunteer roster with everyone
  • 🔸Logistics check — mats, AV, water, signage
🎯
Phase 5 — Conducting
Programme days
▼
  • 🔹Arrive 90 min early — hall setup, AV test, welcome desk
  • 🔹Check-in participants — mark attendance
  • 🔹Receive & brief teacher / facilitator on arrival
  • 🔹Manage session timing, silence, no disturbances
  • 🔹Handle walk-in payments — cash and UPI
  • 🔹First-aid kit on standby
✅
Phase 6 — Windup
Post programme
▼
  • 🔸Collect feedback before participants leave
  • 🔸Send thank-you message within 24 hrs
  • 🔸Follow up with no-shows
  • 🔸Finance reconciliation — submit collection report
  • 🔸Update participant database
  • 🔸Restore venue; return all equipment

Volunteer Education & Orientation

What every volunteer must know before their first programme assignment

📖
Programme Knowledge
Core must-knows
▼
  • 🔹Know programme name, format, duration, target audience
  • 🔹Know eligibility — prerequisite programmes if any
  • 🔹Be able to answer: "What will I experience?"
  • 🔹Know programme fee and all payment modes
  • 🔹Know what participants should bring / wear / avoid
  • 🔹Refer content queries to coordinator — do not guess
🎭
Role Clarity & Boundaries
Escalation path
▼
  • 🔹Know your exact role — stay in lane
  • 🔹Sector Coord → Centre OC → Supporting → Executing
  • 🔹Know who to escalate to for medical / financial / conflict issues
  • 🔹Your energy and presence sets the tone for participants
  • 🔹Dress code — comfortable, modest, Isha-appropriate
💬
Communication Standards
With participants
▼
  • 🔹Always greet with "Namaskaram" — warm, not stiff
  • 🔹Speak clearly and concisely
  • 🔹Do not promote anything beyond the current programme
  • 🔹Complaint? Acknowledge, don't argue; involve coordinator
  • 🔹WhatsApp to participants — only what coordinator authorises
🛠️
Tools & Processes
Trackers, sheets, forms
▼
  • 🔹Know how to use the participant registration tracker
  • 🔹Know how to mark attendance
  • 🔹Know how to log a payment (cash and UPI)
  • 🔹Know how to fill the post-programme summary form
  • 🔹Practice all tools before programme day
